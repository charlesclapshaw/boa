The Boat Owners Association of NSW

For over 40 years, the BOA has been looking after the interests of boat owners across NSW.
‍
We invite you to join us!

The BOA is your representative on the waterways of NSW.Covering issues including Mooring Reform, Abandoned Boats, Boating Charges, Wind Farms and their navigational impact and much more.

We're here to ensure that the interests of boaters are uppermost in the minds of our decision makers.​
