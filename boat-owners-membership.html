About The Boat Owners Association of NSW

Our mission is to ensure that key decision makers in government and the bureaucracy understand the views of boat owners when they are making decisions on a range of issues which affect our ability to peacefully enjoy a day out on the water.

The Boat Owners Association of NSW is dedicated to representing the interests of boat owners across our great state. ​

In concert with collegiate organisations like the BIA and Recreational Fishers Association, its our goal to make sure that the boating voice is heard loud and clear.​

If there are issues that we can assist with, please don't hesitate to contact us!!

Frequently Asked Questions

If you are a BOATER, you simply MUST BE a MEMBER of the BOA

WHO AND WHAT IS THE BOA?
  • The peak body representing recreational boaters in NSW
  • NSW based non-profit organisation founded in 1987
  • Committee elected annually from open membership
  • No political affiliations
WHAT ARE THE ISSUES
  • Navigation safety
  • Sewerage and pump outBoat and dinghy storageGovernment fees and charges
  • Boating facilities
  • Launching ramps and parking
  • Channel dredging
  • Moorings
  • Boater education
WHAT ARE MEMBER BENEFITS
  • You have ready access to an expert committee prepared to act on boating issues
  • You are aware of matters affecting boaters and the action taken by the BOA through our regular Newsletters
  • You have ready access to an expert committee prepared to act on boating issues to promote the maximum enjoyment for responsible boating
  • BOA works hard for recreational boaters
